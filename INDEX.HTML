About

Waypoint Actions

Determines navigation style. Flyover forces a stop/start at the exact center, while Flyby allows the craft to curve through the point to maintain speed.

Timing & Holds

Only active when the action is set to Hold. This defines exactly how many seconds the drone will hover in place before moving to the next mission segment.

Flight Patterns

The drone will circle or loop at the coordinate. Orbit creates a circular path, while Figure 8 creates a double-loop for area coverage.
